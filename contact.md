## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I join the club?

We hold tryouts annually, typically in [Month]. Visit our Teams page for more information about the tryout process and to register when tryouts are announced.

### What age groups do you serve?

We currently offer teams for ages 10-18 (10U through 18U age divisions).

### Do you offer financial assistance?

Yes, we have a limited number of scholarships available based on financial need. Contact us to learn more about the application process.

### Can my child join mid-season?

Team rosters are generally set after tryouts. However, we occasionally have openings due to unforeseen circumstances. Contact us to inquire about availability.

### What is the time commitment?

This varies by team level. Generally, teams practice 2-3 times per week and compete in tournaments on weekends. The season runs from November through June.

### Where do you practice?

We practice at [Facility Name and Address]. Some teams may also use satellite facilities depending on court availability.
