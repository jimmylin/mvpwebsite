Join us for an exciting week of volleyball training! Our Summer Volleyball Camp is designed to improve fundamental skills, introduce game strategies, and provide plenty of competitive playing time.

## Camp Details

**Time:** 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM (Extended care available)  
**Ages:** 10-17 years old  
**Skill Level:** All levels welcome

## What to Expect

### Daily Schedule

- **9:00-10:30**: Skill Stations (passing, setting, hitting)
- **10:30-11:00**: Break & Snack
- **11:00-12:00**: Position-Specific Training
- **12:00-1:00**: Lunch
- **1:00-2:00**: Game Strategy & Drills
- **2:00-3:00**: Tournament Play

### Skills Covered

- Proper passing technique
- Overhead and jump setting
- Approach and hitting mechanics
- Serving (float and jump serve)
- Defensive positioning and digging
- Blocking fundamentals
- Team communication

## What to Bring

- Athletic shoes (volleyball shoes recommended)
- Comfortable athletic clothing
- Water bottle
- Lunch and snacks
- Kneepads (required)
- Athletic tape if needed

## Camp Benefits

- Low coach-to-player ratio (1:8)
- Individual feedback and evaluation
- Video analysis opportunities
- Camp t-shirt included
- End-of-camp certificate
- Competitive games and tournaments

Early bird pricing available until May 15th. Limited spots available, so register early to secure your spot!
