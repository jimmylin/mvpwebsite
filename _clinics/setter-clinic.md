This specialized clinic is designed for setters who want to elevate their game. Over two intensive Saturday sessions, players will work on all aspects of setting technique, decision-making, and leadership.

## Clinic Details

**Time:** 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM each day  
**Ages:** 12-18 years old  
**Skill Level:** Intermediate to Advanced

## Curriculum

### Week 1: Technical Foundation
- Hand positioning and contact
- Footwork and body positioning
- Quick sets and back sets
- Jump setting technique
- Setting under pressure

### Week 2: Game Application
- Reading the pass and adjusting
- Shot selection and distribution
- Communication with hitters
- Running the offense
- Situational decision-making

## What Sets This Clinic Apart

- **Position-Specific Focus**: Exclusively for setters
- **Game Situation Training**: Real-game scenarios and pressure situations
- **Video Review**: Film your sets and receive detailed feedback
- **Guest Coach**: Features a guest appearance by a college setter
- **Small Group Size**: Maximum 16 participants for personalized attention

## Prerequisites

Players should have:
- Basic setting technique
- Experience setting in games
- Commitment to the setter position
- Coachable attitude

## What to Bring

- Volleyball shoes
- Athletic clothing
- Water bottle
- Notebook and pen for taking notes
- Kneepads

## Coach Bio

This clinic will be led by Coach Emily Chen, who played setter at Division I University and has 8 years of coaching experience. She specializes in developing high-level setters and has helped numerous players earn college scholarships.
