## Tournament Travel

As a competitive club, we travel to tournaments throughout the region and sometimes nationally. This page provides important information for families about tournament travel.

## 2024-2025 Tournament Schedule

### Local/Regional Tournaments (within 3 hours)
These tournaments typically don't require overnight stays:

- **December 7-8**: Winter Kickoff Classic (City Convention Center)
- **January 18-20**: MLK Invitational (Regional Sports Complex)
- **February 15-17**: Presidents Day Challenge (University Sports Center)

### Travel Tournaments (requiring overnight stay)
Families will need to arrange hotel accommodations:

- **March 14-16**: Spring Nationals Qualifier (Chicago, IL)
- **April 11-13**: Regional Championships (Cincinnati, OH)
- **June 19-22**: National Championships (Orlando, FL)

## Travel Policies

### Transportation
- **Local Tournaments**: Families are responsible for transportation
- **Travel Tournaments**: Team may organize carpools; families can also drive separately
- **Air Travel**: For national tournaments, families arrange their own flights

### Accommodations
- The club will secure a team hotel block at discounted rates
- Booking deadline information will be sent 6-8 weeks before tournament
- Some tournaments require staying at specific hotels
- Room assignments: typically 2-3 players per room with parent chaperones

### Meals
- Teams often eat together for team bonding
- Some hotels include breakfast
- Tournament venues usually have concessions
- Coach will coordinate team dinners when appropriate

## Cost Expectations

Travel expenses are in addition to team fees and typically include:

- **Hotel**: $100-200 per night depending on location
- **Meals**: $30-50 per day
- **Transportation**: Gas, parking, or airfare
- **Miscellaneous**: Snacks, entertainment, emergencies

Budget approximately:
- **Regional Tournaments**: $200-400 per family
- **National Tournaments**: $800-1,500 per family

## Travel Tips

### Packing Essentials
- Team uniform and practice gear
- Extra athletic clothes
- Comfortable shoes for off-court
- Phone chargers and entertainment
- Snacks and water bottles
- First aid supplies
- Homework (if applicable)

### Hotel Behavior
- Respect quiet hours (10 PM - 7 AM)
- Keep rooms clean and orderly
- No room hopping after curfew
- Report any issues to coaches immediately
- Parents are responsible for supervising their rooms

### Tournament Day
- Arrive at venue 60 minutes before first match
- Bring healthy snacks and plenty of water
- Dress professionally when not playing
- Support teammates who are playing
- Stay with the team between matches

## Parent Volunteer Opportunities

- Driving for local tournaments

- Helping with team meals
- Scorekeeping or line judging
- Setting up team areas
