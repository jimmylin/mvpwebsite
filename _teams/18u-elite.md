Our 18U Elite team is our most competitive squad, comprised of top-level athletes preparing for college volleyball. This team competes in premier tournaments across the region and nationally.

## Team Goals

- Compete at the highest level of club volleyball
- Prepare players for college recruitment
- Develop advanced skills and game strategy
- Foster leadership and teamwork

## Tournament Schedule

- December: Winter Kickoff Classic
- January: MLK Invitational
- February: Presidents Day Challenge
- March: Spring Nationals Qualifier
- April: Regional Championships
- June: AAU/USAV Nationals

## Player Expectations

Players on this team are expected to:

- Attend all practices and tournaments
- Maintain academic eligibility
- Demonstrate leadership and positive attitude
- Commit to individual skill development
- Represent the club with professionalism
