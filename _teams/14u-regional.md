Our 14U Regional team focuses on skill development while providing competitive tournament experiences. This is an excellent opportunity for players to transition from recreational to club volleyball.

## Team Goals

- Master fundamental skills
- Develop volleyball IQ and court awareness
- Build confidence in competitive settings
- Create a positive team culture

## Tournament Schedule

- December: Holiday Tournament
- January: Winter Classic
- February: Valentine's Invitational
- March: Spring Fling
- April: Regional Championships

## Player Expectations

Players on this team should:

- Attend practices consistently
- Show improvement and effort
- Support teammates
- Have fun while learning
